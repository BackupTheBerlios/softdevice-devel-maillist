G550/G450 tv-out (2nd head connector):
RGB:
	Used pins are R(1), G(2), B(3), RGB_SWITCH(9) via 75Ohm resistor,
	Composit(14) via 680Ohm

Composite + Y/C:
	Composite(1), Y(2), C(3)

G400:
RGB:
	R(1), G(2), B(3), Composite(4), RGB_Enable(13)
