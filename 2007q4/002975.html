> # cat /etc/directfbrc

720x576 is mostly used for TV-out. But I guess you are trying vga-out.
So your /etc/fb.modes should have a mode that fits the resolution
specified here.

> depth=32
> pixelformat=ARGB
> disable-module=cle266
> no-vt
> 
> # LANG=c ./vdr -P"softdevice -vo dfb:cle266" -v /tmp
